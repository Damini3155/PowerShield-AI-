Electricity Usage Rules & Guidelines

⚡

Regular Meter Reading

Submit accurate meter readings monthly. Tampering with meters or bypassing them is illegal and will result in severe penalties including fines and legal action.

🔌

Authorized Connections Only

Only use electricity through authorized connections. Unauthorized tapping or illegal connections are considered theft and are strictly prohibited.

📊

Report Anomalies

If you notice sudden spikes or drops in your consumption that cannot be explained, report them immediately to your utility provider for investigation.

🛡️

Protect Your Meter

Keep your electricity meter accessible for inspection. Do not obstruct, damage, or tamper with metering equipment. Report any damage immediately.

💡

Energy Conservation

Use energy-efficient appliances, turn off lights when not in use, and optimize usage during off-peak hours to reduce consumption and costs.

⚠️

Consequences of Theft

Electricity theft is a criminal offense punishable by law. Penalties include heavy fines, disconnection of service, and potential imprisonment.
